About me

Hello, I am Carlos. I am a 25 year old graduate from California State University San Marcos. I am passionate about technology and look forward to contributing to a future where technology can improve the lives of more people.

I was born in Escondido, California and have lived there all of my life. Growing up my favorite school subject was always mathematics and I originally enrolled to CSU San Marcos as a business major. After a couple years as a business major I took a course in computer science and instantly fell in love. I realized at that moment that mathematics is not my passion but problem solving.

Fast forward two years and I became the first person in my family to go to a university and receive a degree. Since the start of the pandemic I have been learning new technologies and languages to become a more well rounded developer. As a father and an older brother, I have a strong drive to be successful for those that look up to me.

I am currently residing in Tokyo, Japan, where I am searching for an entry-level software engineering position.
